    How Blissful Is Luke Bryan Married Life With Wife?

    Unlike other celebrities, ‘American Idol’ judge Luke is open about his personal life. To reveal you the truth, the forever-young looking gentleman is a married man.

    Moreover, Luke Bryan married his college sweetheart, Caroline Boyer and is carefree with his wife’s companionship. Bryan who is followed by 4 million people on his Instagram account is now living a blissful married life wife Caroline Boyer. There is not a bit of rumor of dispute and divorce between the husband and wife till date.

    Luke Bryan and wife Caroline Boyer got married to each other on December 8, in the year 2006.  Boyer met him at Georgia Southern University.

    Besides, the two met at a local bar called Dingus Magees. At that time Bryan was a senior whereas, Boyer was a freshman. Bryan shares two children with his wife, Boyer. His sons-Thomas “Bo” Boyer Bryan (born March 18, 2008) and Tatum “Tate” Christopher Bryan (born August 11, 2010).

    Nevertheless, the happy family seems to be unbreakable, and Luke’s married life with his wife and children looks strong and lifelong.

    The tragic death of Luke Bryan’s brother and sister; what’s the cause of their death?

    Luke faced great tragedies followed by the deaths of his sister, brother and his brother-in-law. Luke at the age of 19, in 1996, was ready to move in Nashville.

    But just before moving, his brother Chris died in a car accident. The plans got postponed due to his brother’s death. After a short time, chasing his dreams, he moved into Nashville.

    Unfortunately, his first play at the Grand Ole Opry became the last time that he would ever be with his sister Kelly. Shortly after that in 2013, she died just seven years later after Bryan’s brother’s death. According to his description to the ABC News, the cause of his sister’s death remains unknown.
