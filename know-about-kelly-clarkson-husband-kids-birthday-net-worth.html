    Kelly Clarkson is an American singer and songwriter, who rose to fame after winning the first season of the singing reality show, American Idol in 2002. She now appears as a judge on NBC singing competition, The Voice, since 2018.

    Kelly Clarkson Bio; Birthday, Parents, Height

    The gifted lady was born as Kelly Brianne Clarkson in 1982 to parents, Stephen Michael Clarkson and Jeanne Clarkson. She celebrates her birthday on the 24th of April every year. Her father, Stephen was an engineer and her mother Jeanne was a first grade English teacher.

    Kelly’s parents got divorced when she was six years old. She is the youngest with three siblings; brother named Jason and a sister, Alyssa. She is a decent personality who stands at a height of 5 feet 3 inches (1.61 m).

    Her Acting Career, Net Worth

    Kelly gave an audition for the inaugural season of the ABC reality show, American Idol: The Search for a Superstar in 2002. After winning the first season of the series, she released her debut single, A Moment Like This. The song became the best-selling single of 2002 in the United States.

    Her albums include Thankful (2003), My December (2007), Piece by Piece (2015) and Meaning of Life (2017). She made her movie debut from the musical romantic comedy film, From Justin to Kelly in 2003.

    Kelly appears as a judge on NBC singing show, The Voice since 2018. (Picture: Kelly’s Instagram)

    Kelly is honored with three Grammy Awards, three MTV Video Music Awards, four American Music Awards, and two Academy of Country Music Awards. Through her career span more than a decade, the Texas native has boasted the estimated net worth of $28 million.

    Married To Talent Manager Husband; Kids

    Kelly started dating talent manager Brandon Blackstock, son of her former manager Narvel Blackstock in 2012. Their love started to blossom and after a year of dating, they got married on 20 October 2013. The wedding ceremony was held at Blackberry Farm in Walland, Tennessee.

    Kelly Clarkson with her husband Brandon Blackstock at Billboard Music Awards in 2018. (Picture: People)

    The couple is proud parents to two adorable kids. At first, they welcomed a daughter, River Rose on 12th June 2014 and a son, Remington Alexander on 12th April 2016. Brandon was previously married to Melissa Ashworth and they have a daughter named Savannah and a son named Seth.

    Some Interesting Facts About Kelly Clarkson

    • Kelly is an author of books such as River Rose and the Magical Lullaby and River Rose and the Magical Christmas.
    • She lent her voice in the computer-animated film, The Star and a guest voice role in the Netflix animated series Home: Adventures with Tip & Oh.
    • Her 11 singles reached the Top Twenty and Billboard Hot 100 charts.
