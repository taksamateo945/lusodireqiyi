    Career

    Regarding her career, Vickie Winans is a well-known gospel singer in the United States. Winans became interested in music after marrying Marvin. As a result, she was asked to participate in Winans Part II. This group included BeBe and CeCe Winans, as well as Daniel Winans, Daniel’s brother.

    Nonetheless, the group never took off, as BeBe and CeCe left to join the PTL Singers of The PTL Club, a long-running Christian television show.

    Furthermore, the Winans became the best-selling act on their record label, Light Records, allowing her husband Marvin to secure a recording contract for her. Her solo album, Be Encouraged, was also a major gospel hit when it was released in 1987.

    Despite this, the album included a cover of Dottie Rambo’s “We Shall Behold Him” and “First Trumpet Sound,” a gospelized remake of Gladys Knight & The Pips’ “Midnight Train To Georgia.”

    Be Encouraged was also nominated for a Grammy for Best Contemporary Soul Gospel Album, a Stellar Award for Album of the Year, and an Excellence Award for Best Female Contemporary Artist.
