    Leslie Grace is an American actor, singer, and songwriter most known for her work as a singer, particularly after the release of her self-titled debut album, which garnered her the first of four Latin Grammy nominations. Her first big break came when her cover of “Will You Still Love Me Tomorrow” topped the Billboard Tropical Songs and Billboard Latin Airplay charts, making her the youngest female artist to do so. She was nominated for Best Tropical Female Artist three years in a row, from 2013 to 2015.

    Her acting career began in 2021 when she played Nina Rosario in Jon M. Chu’s film adaption of “In the Heights.” Grace will play the starring character of Barbara Gordon/Batgirl in the upcoming D.C. Extended Universe feature “Batgirl” on HBO Max on July 21, 2021.

    With her rising popularity, she has amassed a sizable fan base, with over 1.1 million followers on her verified Instagram account, @lesliegrace, and over 97k followers on Twitter, @lesliegrace.

    Leslie Grace Biography

    Leslie Grace was born in The Bronx, New York City, on January 7, 1995. Her citizenship is American. Grace is of mixed ancestry and Dominican descent, and her zodiac sign is Capricorn.

    Grace was born in The Bronx as the youngest of six children to Dominican parents Elba Guana (mother) and Francisco Martinez (father). She was reared in Florida after her family relocated when she was a child. Grace has had an interest in singing and songwriting since she was a child, and she credits her family for giving her the confidence to pursue her dream job as a singer.

    Grace attended Indian Ridge Middle School and Western High School in Florida, where she participated in musicals, talent shows, and choral performances. She also speaks English and Spanish fluently.

    Career

    • Leslie Grace began her professional career when she was 14 years old and recorded and released an independent Christian music album.
    • Her popularity skyrocketed once she published a cover of “Will You Still Love Me Tomorrow?” It peaked at number one on both the Billboard Tropical Songs and the Billboard Latin Airplay charts. In 2009, she released her debut album, “Pasión.” In 2013, she launched her self-titled album, “Leslie Grace,” which charted at number five on both the Billboard Latin Albums and the Billboard Tropical Albums charts.
    • Her singing career has earned her multiple nominations, including three Latin Grammy Awards.
    • One of her overseas trips included a performance at the North Sea Jazz Festival on the Caribbean island of Curaçao.
    • In 2016, she was also the captain of the Univision reality show “Va por ti.”
    • The next year, she released the single “Nada de Amor.”
    • In January 2017, she collaborated with the duo Play-N-Skillz, Wisin, and Frankie J on a reggaeton-style cover of Selena’s song “Si una Vez.”
    • She has also worked with the South Korean boy band “Super Junior” on the song “Lo Siento,” which was uploaded on S.M. Entertainment’s YouTube channel on April 12, 2018.
    • Her acting career began when she was cast as Nina Rosario in the film adaptation of Lin-Manuel Miranda’s musical “In the Heights” in 2021. It was released in 2021 and gained widespread acclaim, with many naming it one of the year’s best films.
    • Her biggest break came when it was confirmed that she would play Barbara Gordon / Batgirl in HBO Max’s “Batgirl,” which will premiere on July 21, 2021.
