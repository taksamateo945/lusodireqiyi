    Tyler from Dream Home Makeover: The Contractor Who Steals the Show

    15 Apr 2024, 00:00

    Who is Tyler Farrell?

    Tyler Farrell is the general contractor who works with Shea and Syd McGee, the stars of Netflix’s reality TV renovation show Dream Home Makeover. Tyler, along with the rest of the crew, assists the couple in renovating and transforming their clients’ homes according to their own style and preferences.

    Tyler has been working with the McGees since season 1 of the show, which premiered in October 2020. The couple started working with Tyler two years before the release of the first series, after doing a project together in Park City, Utah. The pair described it as a “great working relationship” on their show, and it then snowballed into working on other projects together

    What does Tyler do?

    Tyler is not only a general contractor for Dream Home Makeover, but he also has his own construction company in Wasatch County, Utah, called Killowen Construction. According to their official website, they specialize in full-build custom homes, as well as additions and remodels in Summit & Wasatch Counties and beyond

    The website also states, “We have curated a business model that lets us work with some of the best subcontractors in the area. We are sure to communicate ahead of time what our expectations are and we are quick to ask them what they will need to meet those expectations. The hands that build your home are chosen by us, and we have made those relationships a high priority. Our goal is to smoothly take your dreams from concept to reality.”

    Tyler also has a professional Instagram page for his company, @killowenconstruction, which has over 108k followers. He posts pictures and videos of his projects, as well as behind-the-scenes glimpses of his work on Dream Home Makeover.

    What is Tyler’s personal life like?

    Tyler is married to Daile Farrell, and they have four children together. Tyler often shares photos of his family on his personal Instagram page, @twfarrell, which has just under 2k followers. He also posts about his hobbies, such as skiing, golfing, and fishing.

    Tyler is a fan favorite among the viewers of Dream Home Makeover, who admire not only his work but also his physique. Some fans have commented on his Instagram posts, asking to see more of his arms on the show. Others have tweeted about their crush on Tyler, calling him the “real star” of the show

    What’s next for Tyler?

    Tyler is currently featured in season 4 of Dream Home Makeover, which was released on Netflix on December 9, 2022. In the last episode of the season, Tyler reveals his love of bidets, which he installs in one of the clients’ bathrooms. He also jokes about his rivalry with Syd, who often teases him about his work.

    Tyler has not watched the show yet, as he confessed in a video on his company’s Instagram account. He told his fans to go and watch the new season, and thanked them for their support. He also said that he is looking forward to working on more projects with the McGees in the future.

    Tyler from Dream Home Makeover is a talented and hard-working contractor, who has a passion for building and renovating homes. He is also a devoted husband and father, who enjoys spending time with his family and friends. He is a charming and charismatic personality, who has won the hearts of many fans of the show. Tyler is definitely someone to watch out for in the world of home improvement.
