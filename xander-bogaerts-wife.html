    Xander Bogaerts Bio & Family

    The player was born as Xander Jan Bogaerts on 1 October 1992 in Oranjestad, Aruba to Jan and Sandra Bogaerts. His father left the family when he and his twin brother, Jair was at the age of three. The Sox star stands at the height of 6 feet 1 inch (1.93 m) tall and weighs around 95 kg.
