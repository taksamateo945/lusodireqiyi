    Not exactly a scandalous request! Scott Foley recently sat down with Us Weekly exclusively in New York City to reveal his wish for Father’s Day, and he told Us that he is hardly shooting for the moon.

    “Being able to be in the bathroom without anybody coming in,” Foley, 42, said of his hopes for the day. “Is that weird? Is that too simple?”

    “I just want a little stall, that’s all I want!” the Scandal star added. “I just want a moment with like, a magazine and nobody knocking. My 6-month-old does not knock on the door yet. I’m never without child. I have kids everywhere. They’re coming out of every orifice, all the time.”

    The Felicity alum is now the father to three children with his second wife, Marika Dominczyk. Foley, who was previously married to Jennifer Garner for a few years in the early 2000s, welcomed his first child, daughter Malina, in 2009.

    The 5-year-old little girl is the big sister to brother Keller, 2, and baby brother Konrad, whom Foley and Dominczyk welcomed this past November. 

    “It’s terrifying,” Foley told Us this past December of being a parent. “It should scare the hell out of you. But it’s amazing. There’s nothing else I can think of that has that juxtaposition of being at once terrifying and the worst thing you can do for your happy life and the best thing you can do for your happy life to make it even better.”
