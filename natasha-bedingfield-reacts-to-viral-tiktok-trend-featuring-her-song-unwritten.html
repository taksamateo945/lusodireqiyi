    Natasha Bedingfield Reacts to Viral TikTok Trend Featuring Her Song 'Unwritten'

    30 Jul 2024, 00:00

    Natasha Bedingfield is a huge hit on TikTok!

    The British singer-songwriter’s 2004 track “Unwritten” has been going viral on the social media app, thanks in part to two dances to a remix of the song, one by @rony_boyy and the other from user @gleefuljhits.

    Natasha decided to hop on the trend herself, duetting one of the videos with a clip of her trying out the dance.

    In the TikTok, the “Pocket Full of Sunshine” singer wears a neon yellow track suit, rocking back and forth in front of the camera. The video then cuts to her twerking with her sweatpants hanging low.

    She captioned the clip, “here goes ! ?? love love loving this dance you did,” along with some heart emojis.

    Natasha‘s video was a huge hit, garnering over 3.7 million views in less than 24 hours!
