    Jason Kelce is a Center and Plays for the Philadelphia Eagles

Kelce’s athleticism and quickness, combined with his intelligence and strong technique, have made him a leader on the field. However, he worked hard and developed into one of the best centers in the league. He was named to his first Pro Bowl in 2014 and has since been selected to the Pro Bowl every year from 2016 to 2021, making him a six-time Pro Bowl selection.

    Travis Kelce is a Tight End for the Kansas City Chiefs

    As we have already explained, Travis Kelce is a tight end playing for the Kansas City Chiefs in the National Football League (NFL). He was selected by the Kansas City Chiefs in the third round of the 2013 NFL Draft and has since become one of the most accomplished tight ends in the league. Just like his brother, Travis is also a Super Bowl champion, having won Super Bowl LIV with the Chiefs over the San Francisco 49ers and Super LVII with the Chiefs over his brother’s team, the Philadelphia Eagles.

    Travis Kelce played college football at the University of Cincinnati and is considered one of the greatest tight ends of all time. His impressive achievements include 8 Pro Bowl selections, four first-team All-Pro selections, and being named to the NFL 2010s All-Decade Team. Kelce also holds multiple NFL records for tight ends, including the most consecutive and overall seasons with 1,000 yards receiving, the most receiving yards in a single season by a tight end, and becoming the fastest tight end to reach 10,000 career receiving yards.

    In 2020, Travis Kelce set the record for the most receiving yards by a tight end in a single season with 1,416 yards despite playing in only 15 games. He briefly held the single-season record in 2018 before it was broken later that same day. Travis Kelce’s consistent performance and record-breaking achievements have made him one of the most dominant and revered tight ends in the NFL.

    Jason Kelce and Travis Kelce are the First Brothers to Play in the Super Bowl

    The Kelce brothers, Jason and Travis, have made history in the NFL in several ways.     This feat is incredible as it has never happened before in the history of the NFL. This unique feat highlights their talent and dedication to the sport, as well as the support and influence of their family.
