    Lionel Messi has endured a roller-coaster summer as he won his first major title with Argentina and also left Barcelona on a free transfer.

    The 34-year-old made his Paris Saint Germain debut after coming on for Neymar in the 66th minute of their 2-0 triumph over Reims last weekend.

    Meanwhile, while the Argentine was busy at Stade Auguste Delaune, he was given a shout-out by American rapper Kanye West during a listening party for his new album, Donda.

    The six-time Ballon d'Or winner is considered the greatest footballer of all time by many after his long-term achievement in the sport.

    Lionel Messi makes PSG debut against Reims

    In other news, Briefly News previously reported that Messi finally made his Paris Saint Germain debut in the 2-0 win over Reims at Stade Auguste Delaune over the weekend.

    The six-time Ballon d'Or winner came on for his former teammate Neymar in the 66th minute of the encounter.

    His introduction was welcome by about six thousand travelling fans who watched the team claim its fourth straight league win this campaign.
