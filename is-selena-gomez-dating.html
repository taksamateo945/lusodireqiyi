    Is Selena Gomez Dating? A Look at Her Love Life in 2023

    Selena Gomez is one of the most popular and successful singers and actresses in the world.

    She has millions of fans who are curious about her personal life, especially her love life.

    Is Selena Gomez dating anyone in 2023? Who are her ex-boyfriends and what happened to them?

    Here’s everything you need to know about Selena Gomez’s dating history and current relationship status.

    Selena Gomez and Zayn Malik: The Latest Rumor

    The most recent rumour about Selena Gomez’s love life is that she is dating former One Direction member Zayn Malik.

    The speculation started in March 2023, when the two were spotted having dinner together in New York City.

    A few weeks later, Selena followed Zayn on Instagram, and he followed her back.

    Fans went wild with theories that the two were secretly dating, especially since Zayn had split from his longtime girlfriend Gigi Hadid in February 2023.

    However, in June 2023, Selena finally addressed the rumours with a hilarious TikTok video.

    In the clip, she is seen watching a soccer game and yelling at the players,

    “I’m single! I’m just a little high maintenance, but I’ll love you so much.”

    She captioned the video,

    “The struggling man lol,” which featured one of her friends laughing in the background.

    So, it seems that Selena and Zayn are just friends, and she is not dating anyone right now.

    Selena Gomez and The Weeknd: The Ex That Got Away

    One of Selena Gomez’s most famous ex-boyfriends is The Weeknd, whose real name is Abel Tesfaye.

    The two dated for 10 months in 2017, after he broke up with his on-and-off girlfriend Bella Hadid.

    Selena and The Weeknd had a whirlwind romance that took them around the world, as she joined him on his Starboy: Legend of the Fall tour.

    They also made their red carpet debut as a couple at the Met Gala in May 2017.

    However, their relationship ended in October 2017, reportedly due to their busy schedules and long distance.

    A source told PEOPLE at the time,

    “She and Abel have been going back and forth for a few months about their relationship. It’s been hard with him being on tour and her shooting in New York.”

    Shortly after their breakup, Selena was seen hanging out with her ex Justin Bieber again, while The Weeknd rekindled his romance with Bella Hadid.

    In 2020, The Weeknd released his album After Hours, which featured several songs that seemed to be about his relationship with Selena.

    For example,

    • in “Save Your Tears,” he sings, “I broke your heart like someone did to mine / And now you won’t love me for a second time.
    • ” In “Heartless,” he raps, “Never need a bitch, I’m what a bitch need / Tryna find the one that can fix me.”
    • In “Blinding Lights,” he croons, “I said ooh I’m blinded by the lights / No I can’t sleep until I feel your touch.”

    Selena Gomez and Justin Bieber: The On-and-Off Saga

    The most iconic and turbulent relationship in Selena Gomez’s dating history is undoubtedly with Justin Bieber.

    The two met as teenagers in 2009 and started dating in 2010.

    They were on and off for eight years, breaking up and making up several times.

    They had their fair share of drama, including cheating allegations, social media feuds, and public breakups.

    Their last reunion was in 2018, after Selena’s kidney transplant surgery due to her lupus condition.

    Justin was supportive of her recovery and they rekindled their romance for a few months.

    However, they broke up again in March 2018, and Justin moved on quickly with model Hailey Baldwin.

    He proposed to Hailey in July 2018 and married her in September 2018.

    Selena was heartbroken by Justin’s marriage and expressed her feelings in her songs “Lose You to Love Me” and “Look at Her Now” from her 2020 album Rare.

    In “Lose You to Love Me,” she sings, “In two months you replaced us / Like it was easy / Made me think I deserved it / In the thick of healing.”

    In “Look at Her Now,” she sings, “Of course she was sad / But now she’s glad she dodged a bullet / Took a few years to soak up the tears / But look at her now.”

    Conclusion

    Selena Gomez has had several high-profile relationships over the years, but none of them lasted.

    She has been single for over two years now and seems to be happy with herself.

    Selena has focused on her music, her acting, her beauty line, and her mental health.

    She has also been open about her dating struggles and her desire to find true love.
