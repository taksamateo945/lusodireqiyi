    American king of RnB and soul Justin Timberlake is a father for the second time.

    The musician and his wife Jessica Biel welcomed their child, a son, and he confirmed the news on Monday, January 18.

    The pair had been quiet throughout their pregnancy journey though most of their fans had already figured out their second child was on the way.

    The 39-year-old revealed the good news during an interview with comedian and talk show host Ellen DeGeneres.

    Justin said that his second son’s name is Phineas who happens to be a loud crier.

    “Yeah, I'll tell you his name. His name is Phineas. He's awesome and he's so cute and nobody's sleeping,” said the happy father of two.

    The Mirrors singer stated that despite the sleepless night, he and his better half are stoked to be parents for the second time.

    They love the latest addition to their family and are extremely proud to be his parents.

    “But we're thrilled. We're thrilled and couldn't be happier. Very grateful,” the musician added.

    In other news, We reported that Kim Kardashian and Kanye West's divorce drama continues with news that the couple have stopped attending marriage counselling.

    A source confirmed to People magazine that the celebrity couple were no longer in therapy and that a public announcement is fast-approaching.

    Kim and Kanye reportedly began attending therapy to sort through issues in their relationship but the source has confirmed that the couple is no longer attending sessions.

    "They are just not on the same page when it comes to their future as a family. And Kim is okay with it."

    Most have taken this latest development as confirmation that a public announcement of their split is not far behind.

    Fans believe the latest development is confirmation that neither Kim nor Kanye see a way that can salvage their relationship.
