    12 Feb 2024, 00:00

    Lil Nas X is pulling a costume change on the 2021 BET Awards red carpet!

    The 22-year-old “Old Town Road” rapper first walked the red carpet in a pink and blue dress, before changing into a floral-print suit for his second trip down the carpet on Sunday (June 27) at the Microsoft Theater in Los Angeles.

    PHOTOS: Check out the latest pics of Lil Nas X

    While not nominated at the show tonight, Lil Nas X will be performing his hit song, “Call Me By Your Name (Montero)” during the show.

    Other stars in attendance included DJ Khaled, Fat Joe, Jack Harlow, Roddy Ricch, DaBaby, Migos, and Lil Uzi Vert with girlfriend JT.

    The 2021 BET Awards, hosted by Taraji P. Henson, are airing RIGHT NOW on BET.

    FYI: Lil Nas X‘s suit is by Richard Quinn. Migos is wearing outfits by Bottega Veneta. Roddy is wearing Bottega Veneta.
